What we offer

Let us help you stand up for your health.

Choose Right Shoes

Wearing comfortable shoes can effectively reduce the pressure on the feet and legs, slow down the fatigue of the lower limbs.

Be Healthy Weight

Obesity is one of the important reasons for increasing the burden on the body and exacerbate varicose veins, especially for people who need to stand for a long time at work.

Set Break Time

Taking rest breaks during work can help to prevent leg swelling or fatigue and also decrease the risks of long-term symptom such as varicose veins.

Do Exercise

Exercise is one of the best ways to reduce the physical harm caused by prolonged standing and it can also play a good preventive role from symptoms of prolonged standing .

Workplace Support

Organization of the work space is another important aspect. The worker should have the opportunity to change their body positions frequently.

Do Exercise Plan

Are you too lazy to do exercise? A weekly exercise plan can help you. We have selected exercises that can reduce symptoms of prolonged standing for you.
